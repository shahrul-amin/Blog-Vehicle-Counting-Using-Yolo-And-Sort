Our Stories

Shahrul Amin

Working on this project has been a highly educational experience, enhancing my understanding of combining the YOLO and SORT algorithms for real-time object tracking. I gained insights into the YOLO algorithm, particularly the functionality of its backbone, Darknet-53. Additionally, this project helped me sharpen my web development skills through the creation of a blog, which allowed me to effectively document and share our progress. Overall, this project enriched my knowledge in computer vision and web development, providing a practical application for my technical skills.

Muhammad Marzlan

In this project, I learned about the significant role AI and Machine Learning play in the global digital market and future development. Specifically, I explored how AI can manage road traffic by detecting car speeds, counting vehicles, and predicting congestion. Additionally, I discovered the benefits of using AI for video presentations, which helped me overcome my challenges with public speaking. Working on this project also highlighted the importance of communication and teamwork for success. Overall, this experience has deepened my understanding of AI and its potential to improve various aspects of our lives.

Afif Iqbal

This process improved my skills in promotion and client attraction, which will be valuable for future projects. Additionally, working on this project broadened my perspective on AI, showing me how this advanced technology can solve complex problems like traffic congestion.
