Understanding the AI Algorithm

Overview

Our traffic management system uses a combination of YOLO (You Only Look Once) and SORT (Simple Online and Realtime Tracking) algorithms for real-time vehicle detection and tracking.

YOLO (You Only Look Once)

YOLO is a state-of-the-art, real-time object detection system. It divides the image into a grid and predicts bounding boxes and probabilities for each region. This allows it to detect multiple vehicles in a single frame efficiently.

Read More

SORT (Simple Online and Realtime Tracking)

SORT is an efficient algorithm for tracking multiple objects in real-time. It uses the bounding boxes from YOLO to track the movement of vehicles across frames, providing a reliable count of vehicles.

Benefits of Our Algorithm

Accuracy

Our AI algorithm provides high accuracy in vehicle detection and counting, even in complex traffic scenarios.

Real-Time Processing

The integration of YOLO and SORT ensures that our system can process video feeds in real-time, making it ideal for dynamic traffic environments.

Scalability

Our solution is scalable and can be deployed across various intersections and roadways, providing a comprehensive traffic management system.

Cost-Effective

By utilizing advanced AI algorithms, we reduce the need for expensive hardware and infrastructure, making our solution cost-effective.
